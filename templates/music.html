Between October 1966 and November 1967, the Monkees put out four albums. All four went to No. 1. The band had six Top 5 singles - “Daydream Believer” has been recorded by a wide range of musicians, including Paul Westerberg of the Replacements, the Four Tops, Britpop act Robson & Jerome, dance pop singers Atomic Kitten, Canadian songstress Ann Murray and Japanese all-girl rockers Shonen Knife.

The Monkees use a strong rhythm section of double bass and drums as the anchor for a lead section of brass instruments such as trumpets and trombones. Their music creates a "visceral response" such as feet-tapping or head-nodding to get everyone swinging and dancing the night away!
